Abraham Lincoln

Abraham Lincoln was one of the greatest US presidents ever. He had many accomplishments that were unfathomed before his time. He is most famous for writing the emancipation proclamation, which freed slaves all throughout the US.

Here is a timeline of his life

  • 1832-Runs for first Political Office and loses
  • 1836-Became a Lawyer
  • 1842-Gets Married
  • 1846-Gets elected for the house of representatives
  • 1860-Wins Presidency
  • 1864-Signs Emancipation Proclamation
  • 185-Assassinated by John Wilkes Booth
